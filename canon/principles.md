## Foundational commitments

1. **Welfare assessment centres on Affective Experiences.**
   Physical or behavioural indicators of welfare are proxies; the morally relevant
   quantity is the affective experience of the individual animal.

2. **The WFF causal chain is preserved throughout all assessments.**
   Circumstances → Biological Consequences → Affective Experiences → Welfare Metrics.
   Each level is distinct. Conflating levels is a methodological error.

3. **Valence and intensity are disaggregated.**
   Primary WFF outputs preserve disaggregation by valence and intensity
   category. Cumulative Pain and Cumulative Pleasure are reported separately.
   Cumulative Pain remains disaggregated by Pain intensity category, and
   Cumulative Pleasure remains disaggregated by Pleasure intensity category.
   Pain and Pleasure are not netted in primary WFF outputs. Intensity
   categories are not collapsed into a single scalar primary output. Any
   scalar transformation, netting, weighting, value-weighted welfare score,
   or cross-category collapse is an external downstream transformation, not
   a primary WFF output, and must not be presented as one.

4. **Assumptions are made explicit.**
   No assumption is hidden. Where values are uncertain, uncertainty is quantified
   or bounded, not suppressed.

5. **Open questions are not treated as settled.**
   Unresolved methodological issues are documented in `review/` and are clearly
   marked as open. They are not silently resolved by authorial fiat.

6. **WFI provides evidence; WFI does not issue moral verdicts.**
   WFI delivers transparent metrics, structured comparisons, and explicit
   assumptions. Moral and policy conclusions are the responsibility of
   decision-makers, not WFI as an institution.

7. **Scientific rigor takes precedence over operational convenience.**
   Simplifications made for practical reasons must be documented and their
   directional effects on estimates noted.
