# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

This repository hosts the scientific canon of the Welfare Footprint Institute
(WFI). Contributors, reviewers, and maintainers are expected to keep
interactions respectful, evidence-focused, and constructive.

## Expected behaviour

- Engage in **respectful technical and scientific disagreement.** Critique
  ideas, methods, and evidence — not people.
- Be **clear about evidence and uncertainty.** Distinguish established findings
  from assumptions, open questions, and provisional content. Do not present
  unreviewed claims as settled.
- Assume good faith, and give others the benefit of careful reading before
  responding.
- Accept that scientific questions are resolved through review, not through
  insistence or volume of comments.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Demeaning, discriminatory, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or the review process.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable
behaviour and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to
behaviour they deem inappropriate. This may include editing or removing
comments, issues, and pull request content, or temporarily or permanently
restricting a contributor's participation.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the maintainers at:

**wladimir.j.alonso@welfarefootprint.org**

information (such as inadvertently exposed credentials or private material),
follow `SECURITY.md` instead of opening a public issue.
